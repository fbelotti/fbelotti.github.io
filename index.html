Federico Belotti

Associate Professor of Econometrics

Department of Economics and Finance

University of Rome Tor Vergata

Welcome! I am an (applied) econometrician. My job is to develop new statistical tools for the quantitative analysis of actual economic phenomena. I am especially interested to panel/clustered data, robust estimation and getting more and more passionate about spatial and network econometrics. I have a broad range of applied interests and enjoy coding econometric tools that may help other researchers in their work.
